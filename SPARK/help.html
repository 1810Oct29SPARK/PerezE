FAQ

Q. My travel plans have changed, can I modify my reservation on-line?
A. Yes, you can do almost everthing online if you so wish. Go to your user account and click on 'cancel reservation' and it will get taken care of with no questions asked.
Q. What is the difference between standard, concierge, Executive, Club and Tower classes?
A. A standard EP hotel offeres you basic room amenities like continenal breakfast, free wifi and free access to the bar. The other three classes give you all the basic accommodations along with access to to different private lounges with an exclusive bar with 5 free margaritas, appetizers and snackes, along with evening hors d'oeuvres and morning margaritas.
Q. What is the AAA rate?
A. The American Automobile Association (AAA) rate is for members of AAA. To get this rate, show your current membership card upon check-in. You must also provide your 16 digit AAA code when making your reservation. Availability of AAA rate varies by hotel.
Q. What is the AARP rate?
A. The American Association of Retired Persons (AARP) rate is for AARP members. To get this rate, show your current membership card upon check-in. Availability of AARP rate varies by hotel.
Q. Why should I create an account? What benefits do I get?
A. You need an account to make a reservation so that we can provide the best possible experience at you time here. Other benefits include easily ordering services and signing up for activities that we offer such as the helicopter tour and private city bus tour. You will also accumulate points which can be used to pay for part of your stay, drinks, food, and various other things.
