Software setup and logic

The program I used to capture an image and proccess the information was Matlab. It is a matrix-based programing language that is mainly used by engineers and scientists for its natural expression of computational mathamatics. Interested in learning more? Want to find out more? Click here.


  1. Program starts
  2. Camera takes initial picture and converts to grey scale
  3. Saves picture and waits for user to place items and hit "continue"
  4. Takes second picture and also turns to grey scale
  5. Subtracts the first picture from the second picture, keeps the difference
  6. Calculates how far the screw is by counting pixels
  7. Calculates distance from pixels to cm
  8. Uses inverse kinematics to figure out how and where to move robotic arm
  9. Sends information to arm
  10. Turns on magnet when in place, picking up screw
  11. Moves to where container is
  12. Turns off magnet, dropping screw
  13. Complete!
